About Me

Software Engineer

Hello! I'm Stefan, a software engineer based in Seattle, WA.

I'm a Chemical Engineer turned Property Loss Control Engineer turned Software Engineer, having worked in industry for 6 years prior to the pursuit of a Master's in Computer Science.

In my previous career working for a large commercial property insurance provider that insures 1 in 3 of the Fortune 1000, I had the privilege to work with many clients from various industries. With that experience, I came to realize that we live in an era where the proliferation of massive amounts of data and information is a reality, and that even traditionally non-tech companies, industries, and marketplaces, have needed to adopt the frameworks and methods of major tech companies. Modern day has come to require modern methods, and everyone is seeking to stay competitive and continue to provide the best services possible with use of software.

As such, I've excitedly entered the world of computer science, where making impact at scale is the norm. True to my engineering background, I enjoy building things, whether that be websites, applications, or back-end solutions, with a goal of providing performant solutions that ultimately solve problems.
